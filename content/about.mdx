I build digital products that need to feel fast, stay maintainable, and hold up under real-world complexity.

My work sits at the intersection of **frontend clarity**, **backend reliability**, and **platform thinking**. I care about systems that are easy to reason about, easy to ship, and strong enough to evolve without collapsing under their own weight.

## What I Build

- **Content-driven platforms** that balance editorial quality with a clear technical architecture
- **Full-stack products** where the UI, API, and deployment workflow feel like part of one system
- **Automation and AI-assisted tools** that reduce repetitive operational work without sacrificing traceability
- **Edge-ready applications** designed for responsiveness, resilience, and straightforward delivery

## How I Work

- **Pragmatic over trendy**: I favor solutions that are robust, testable, and sustainable over novelty for its own sake.
- **Systems first**: I like making sure content models, tooling, and deployment paths support the product instead of fighting it.
- **Readable code matters**: Clean structure and naming are not polish - they are what make teams move faster over time.
- **Iteration with intent**: I prefer shipping a strong version, measuring what matters, and improving from real usage.

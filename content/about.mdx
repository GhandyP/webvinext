## How I Work With You

My process follows a clear cycle designed to deliver value quickly without sacrificing judgment:

1. **Diagnosis**: I understand the real problem, the context, and the failure points before touching a line of code.
2. **Technical design**: I define the architecture clearly so the solution is visible before it’s built.
3. **MVP**: I deliver a first version that is useful, fast, and verifiable.
4. **Iteration**: I adjust based on real data, not assumptions.
5. **Documentation**: I leave decisions, dependencies, and constraints clear.
6. **Deployment**: I close with a foundation that can support growth without becoming fragile.
</Panel>

<WarningPanel>
**What I DON'T do**:
- Quick fixes that break tomorrow
- Undocumented code
- Non-scalable architectures
- Projects without technical clarity from the start
- Products that look good in demo and fail in production

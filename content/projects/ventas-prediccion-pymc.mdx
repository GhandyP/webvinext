## Problem

Sales projections often rely too heavily on simple averages and leave little visibility into uncertainty.

## Approach

I built a Bayesian workflow to capture historical sales, infer the future distribution, and present the results in an app.

## Outcome

The project enables planning with more realistic intervals and a clearer reading of the forecast.

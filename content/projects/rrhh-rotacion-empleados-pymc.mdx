## Problem

Employee turnover is often detected too late, using tools that are too rigid to capture uncertainty.

## Approach

I built a Bayesian model to estimate each employee's likelihood of leaving based on work-context variables.

## Outcome

The system makes it possible to detect turnover risk before the problem escalates and guide more informed retention actions.

## Problem

Traditional organizations model work by job titles, which hides talent and makes it difficult to respond when needs change.

## Approach

I developed a People Analytics framework based on skills to infer capabilities, detect gaps, and prioritize development using Bayesian models.

## Outcome

This work demonstrates how PyMC can support more agile and useful talent decisions compared to a rigid scheme based solely on job titles.

## Problem

The previous portfolio lacked a durable content model and did not communicate technical depth clearly enough. It acted more like a static brochure than a platform that reflects current engineering capability.

## Approach

I rebuilt the site around generated MDX modules instead of runtime frontmatter parsing, strengthened the editorial visual language, kept validation in the content layer for structured publishing, and fixed Worker preview compatibility to reduce platform friction.

## Outcome

The result is not just a nicer portfolio. It is a stronger base for ongoing writing, case studies, and future product experiments with a reliable preview/deploy workflow and clearer engineering narrative.

## Problem

Job satisfaction changes with many variables, and it is worth measuring before it leads to exits or underperformance.

## Approach

I built a Bayesian model that estimates the probability of cases below threshold and shows the result in an app.

## Outcome

The project helps prioritize follow-up and preventive actions.

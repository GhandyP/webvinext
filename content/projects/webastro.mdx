## Problem

The earlier site lacked a durable content model and did not communicate technical depth clearly enough, acting more like a static brochure than a platform reflecting engineering capability.

## Approach

I created a cleaner editorial structure with MDX, established intentional project narratives around problem/role/outcome, introduced reusable project/blog patterns, and set the editorial baseline that now informs the Vinext version's stronger messaging.

## Outcome

Webastro established a durable content model, stronger portfolio voice centered on engineering work, and editorial baseline that informs current Vinext site messaging, while introducing reusable patterns for less ad hoc publishing.
